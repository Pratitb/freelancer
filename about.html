My Story

I have always loved technology. From a young age, technology has interested me. Most children play video games of some kind, from tetris to road rash to shooting games, I loved playing around with computers.

The more I played, the more I wanted to understand how things worked. If something was not working, I would look into it to figure out a way to solve the problem. I still build my own computers

I never lost my curiosity, but my interest in technologies changed as I expanded my knowledge from taking apart computers to building websites in code. Now I focus on people and how does a website bring value to them.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology. I worked on a couple of projects related to web that sparked my interest in web development

I have used this to start my professional career as a developer with expertise in building responsive websites, helping customers reach their goal, bring ideas to life and much more.
